# Website Design & Development Specification for Amaanc.com

**Project:** Amaanc Limited Website  
**Client:** Amaanc Limited  

---

## 1.0 Project Overview

Amaanc is an established consultancy firm (Since 2012) specializing in digital transformation through Salesforce, Artificial Intelligence, and complementary enterprise technologies.

The goal of this project is to design and develop a modern, professional, and lead-generating website at **www.amaanc.com** that effectively communicates Amaanc’s expertise, showcases service offerings, and serves as a primary channel for client engagement.

The website must:
- Reflect Amaanc’s position as an industry leader  
- Highlight decade-plus experience  
- Be built on a flexible, easily manageable platform  

---

## 3.0 Sitemap & Page Specifications

The following pages represent the core site structure. Amaanc reserves the right to add up to **five (5) additional pages** during the project development phase.

- Home  
- Services (with individual sub-pages for each service)  
- Key Features  
- Industries  
- Engage With Us (with sub-pages)  
- Contact Us  

---

## 3.1 Detailed Page Requirements

### 3.1.1 Home Page

**Objective:**  
Create a powerful first impression, clearly communicate Amaanc’s core value proposition, and drive user actions such as **Contact Us** and **View Services**.

**Requirements:**
- Dynamic and visually striking hero section  
- Compelling headline with strong CTA  
- Clear emphasis on **“Since 2012”**  
- Brief introduction to core specialties:
  - Salesforce
  - Artificial Intelligence
  - Integrations
- Sections highlighting:
  - Key services
  - Featured case studies
  - Client testimonials
- Optimized for fast loading speeds  

---

### 3.1.2 Services Page & Sub-Pages

**Objective:**  
Present Amaanc’s complete service portfolio and allow users to easily explore detailed solutions.

#### Parent Page (Services)
- High-level overview of all services  
- Each service displayed as a card or section
- 2–3 sentence summary per service
- “Learn More” link to dedicated sub-page  

#### Service Sub-Pages
Each sub-page must include:
- Detailed service description  
- Business problems solved  
- Amaanc’s tailored approach  
- Relevant technologies  

#### Services Grouping

**Core Expertise**
- Salesforce Consultancy  
- Artificial Intelligence  
- Integrations  

**Enterprise Solutions**
- .NET Consultancy  
- SAP Consultancy  

**Design & Development**
- Android / iOS App Development  
- UI / UX Design  

**Implementation & Support**
- Business Analysis  
- Testing (Automated & Manual)  
- DevOps (CI/CD & Backup)  
- Customer Success  
- Change Management  

**Talent & Growth**
- Training  
- IT Recruitment  

---

### 3.1.3 Key Features Page

**Objective:**  
Showcase Amaanc’s technical proficiency, particularly within the Salesforce Cloud ecosystem.

**Requirements:**
- Highlight expertise in:

  - Service Cloud

### 3.1.5 Engage With Us Section & Sub-Pages

**Objective:**  
Build thought leadership, provide valuable content, and keep audiences engaged.

#### Sub-Pages

**Case Studies**
- Client challenges
- Amaanc’s solutions
- Measurable results  

**Blog**
- CMS-driven  
- Supports categories, tags, and social sharing  
- Regular publishing of insights, trends, and news  

**Newsroom**
- CMS-driven
- Official announcements
- Press releases
- Milestones (partnerships, awards, etc.)

**Resources**
- Secure page (future gated access optional)
- White papers, e-books, reports  

**Events**
- Dynamic calendar view
- Webinars, conferences, speaking engagements  

---

### 3.1.6 Contact Us Page

**Objective:**  
Primary conversion point for lead generation.

**Requirements:**
- User-friendly contact form with fields:
  - Name
  - Company
  - Email
  - Phone
  - Area of Interest (dropdown)
  - Message
- Automated confirmation email to user  
- Form submissions emailed to internal Amaanc address  
- Clearly displayed:
  - Address
  - Phone
  - General email  
- Embedded Google Map (recommended)

**Popup Contact Form**
- Timer-based popup:
  - First popup: 2 minutes
  - Second popup: 5 minutes
  - Third popup: 10 minutes

## 6.0 Functional Requirements

- SEO-friendly architecture
  - Semantic HTML
  - Meta titles & descriptions
  - Alt tags
  - Clean URLs  

- Security best practices implemented  

- Fully responsive (desktop, tablet, mobile)  

- Cross-browser compatibility
  - Chrome
  - Firefox
  - Safari
  - Edge  

- Automatic language translation by region
  - Default: English
  - Example: Spanish for users in Spain  

- Optimized performance
  - Minified CSS/JS
  - Optimized images  

- Cookies consent banner (EU & UK compliance)
  - GDPR
  - ePrivacy Directive
  - PECR
